Opening Night

We open the festival with a meditation on aesthetics. Moving slowly between gallery, studio and Karlaplan we delve into the materiality of time. Witnessing bodies gliding, twitching, meeting and transforming into new structures of matter.

The opening is free of charge and is happening in collaboration with Cristina Caprioli / CCAP who premiers ASKA in the Hall at 19. We start to screen films right after at 20.30 at SITE (right across the square of the Hall).

TALK: Stockholm Conversations, small stage

To start our festival at Dansens Hus we want to have an intimate conversation about dance film and screendance in the Stockholm community. How this resonates locally and on a global level. We invite artists, audiences, and practitioners to be part of the conversation to map the communities and their discourses, movements, and needs of it.

SCREENING and TALK: Section V, small stage

This part of the section is dedicated to David Bloom and his film trilogy "Sex & Space”. We will show his dance movie Shapeshifters. Shapeshifters follow five porn actors in a dance studio busy with movement tasks and dance improvisations while they describe their porn performances. The focus is on the body, its desires and the social stigma around it.

Shapeshifters

David Bloom

DE

21:00

In a moderated talk after the screening, David Bloom will talk about his experience as a filmmaker and choreographer focusing on the representation of the body and its connection to voyeurism and body desires.
